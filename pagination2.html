Pagination

Pagination in HTML refers to the process of dividing content into separate, numbered pages, typically for navigation purposes. It's a common technique used to manage large datasets or content-heavy web pages by breaking them into smaller, more manageable chunks. This improves user experience by reducing loading times and making it easier to browse through the content.
